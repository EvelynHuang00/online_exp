Snack choices

In this part, you will see two snacks on each screen:

Your job is to choose the snack you would rather eat right now.

How to choose

Use the keyboard arrow keys to make your choice.

  • Left snack = press the left arrow key (←)
  • Right snack = press the right arrow key (→)

As soon as you press ← or →, your choice is made and the next screen will appear.

Answer in time

You will have some time to make each choice, but it is not unlimited.

There is usually enough time to decide.

If you do not answer in time, the task will move on to the next screen.

Take a moment

You can use the arrow buttons to review these instruction pages at any time.

Make sure everything is clear before moving on.

When you feel ready, click Next to start a practice round.
