Welcome!

In this study, you will be in a group of 6 people.

At the end, the 6 snacks will be divided so that each person gets 1 snack.

To do this, we first need to learn which snacks you like more.

What you will do

You will do two simple tasks:

  • Bidding: Tell us how much you want each snack.
  • Choosing: Pick the snack you like more when you see two snacks.

These tasks help us learn your snack preferences.

How snacks are assigned

Everyone in your group will do the same tasks.

We will use each person’s answers to work out what snacks they prefer.

Then a computer rule will use those preferences to assign the 6 snacks across the 6 people.

This process includes some randomness.

Take a moment

You can use the arrow buttons to review these instruction pages at any time.

Make sure you understand before moving on.

You will see more detailed instructions in the next steps.

When you feel ready, click Next to continue.
